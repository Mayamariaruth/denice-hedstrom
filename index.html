Who am I?

I am an intuitive Transformational Healer, Self mastery coach and Mentor trained and experienced in alternative healing methods such as Shamanic Healing, Holotropic Breathwork, Inner child work, Energy alchemy as well as a Cert. Ayurvedic Practitioner.

I am experienced with working with over 150+ clients all over the world, of all ages and from different backgrounds with incredible results and shifts and my work all roots from my own experience of healing myself and radically changing my life.

I had my upbringing in Sweden but have traveled and lived abroad for many years, a big part of my journey has been in the land of Bali.

For a long time I have worked intuitively with my own healing, energy, 'the unseen' realms and trauma release through the lens of spirituality, energy and the power of manifestation in the quantum field.

My passion and purpose is serving women who are seeking to deeply heal and transform their lives as they step fully into their potential, purpose, freedom and abundance!

My passion for helping others stems from my own journey of healing myself from childhood trauma, early life abandonment trauma, daily anxiety and toxic patterns in relationships - having transformed self-destructive behavior and constant anxiety into a life of deep self-love, confidence, worthiness, peace and empowerment of the creation of my reality.
